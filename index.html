Fair Housing Compliance Built In

Federal law prohibits describing who a property is "for." ChatGPT writes "perfect for families" and "ideal for retirees" without flagging them. Clear Access never does. Every output is screened against 150+ restricted terms automatically.

Uses "primary suite" automatically. Never references buyer demographics, religion, or neighborhood composition. Your compliance risk drops to near zero.

Price-Tier Psychology

A $280K starter home and a $1.4M estate need completely different copy: different tone, vocabulary, sentence structure, and emotional target. The agent detects price tier and applies the right mode automatically.

Four distinct modes: Entry-Level, Mid-Range, Executive, and Luxury. Each with its own rules. No prompting needed on your end.

Anti-AI Language Enforcement

Generic AI copy is immediately recognizable: "seamlessly," "thoughtfully," "boasting," "meticulously." Buyers and agents spot it instantly and it tanks credibility. Clear Access bans 30+ AI-pattern phrases and enforces the ban on every output.

Zero em dashes. Zero banned openers. The output reads like a skilled human copywriter wrote it on a good day. That is the standard it is held to.

The MLS Short output is built for MLS submission, calibrated to character limits, free of restricted terms, and formatted as clean prose. Not a truncated version of something longer. Built specifically for MLS.
